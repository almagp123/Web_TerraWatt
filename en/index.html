Let us guide you to perfectly control your energy consumption

Personalized Consumption Analysis

Our tool calculates expected electricity consumption based on your province's average consumption and local weather conditions. Every weather data point, from temperature to humidity, is taken into account to ensure accurate forecasting.

Demographic and Household Factors

To create an accurate and personalized estimate of your electricity consumption, we take into account several essential factors of your home. We analyze the number of residents living in the property, which directly impacts the use of appliances and energy demand. Additionally, we consider the type of housing, whether it's a single-family home or an apartment, as each has different consumption patterns due to its structure and insulation. We also include in the analysis the contracted power, which is fundamental to determine the available energy consumption limit, ensuring our estimate is as close as possible to your household's reality.

Cost Prediction and Optimization

Through analysis of patterns and general trends, our system finds opportunities to reduce consumption. Additionally, we help you visualize how these factors will influence your monthly bill, providing tools to optimize your consumption and reduce costs.
